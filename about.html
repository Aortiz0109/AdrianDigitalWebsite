About Me

Hey there! I am Adrian, a digital artist and designer. I have a passion for great design; my whole life, I've been creating art inspired by all sorts of media. After earning a degree in digital art and design from Towson University, I spent 7 years managing a movie theatre in downtown Baltimore before finally returning to art in 2020. As time went on, my combination of art and business knowledge began to turn into other small business opportunities; as friends and family asked for me to design custom websites, marketing materials, and art commissions for them.


Since that time, I have been eagerly freelancing on various projects while continuing to learn all things design, user interface/experience, and programming; in order to build eye-catching, easy-to-use, responsive content that is tailored to my clients' customer base. I’m adaptable and have used tons of various software, depending on what’s useful, with most of my experience now being in the Adobe suite of software, Blender, Figma, and Visual Studio Code for web. I apply everything that I discover into my projects, and I am passionate about doing my very best with each project that I take on.


Recently, I became interested in UX design because over the years, many of my negative experiences in various product designs boiled down to assumptions that designers made without regard for the end-user. So, I enrolled and completed a Google UX Certification course to further learn Google designers' process in user-focused design and its practical applications for businesses.
