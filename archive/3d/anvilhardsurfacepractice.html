Anvil (Hard Surface Practice)

Blender | 2020

After completing the Donut Cafe project, which served as my introduction to Blender, I continued my learning journey by exploring various tutorials. One of these tutorials guided me in creating an Anvil, allowing me to hone my skills in hard surface modeling and texturing within the Blender environment. It was an enjoyable project, and I dedicated time to adding realistic details and weathering effects to enhance the quality of the metal texture.
