Print Magazine

Magazine Cover and Spreads | 2010

Indesign | Photoshop | Illustrator

For my typography class, I undertook the final project which involved designing a print magazine cover, a table of contents, and a two-page spread. Following the theme of Wolfgang Weingart and his Swiss punk style of design, I likely created a collage for the cover using Photoshop and then arranged the layout in InDesign. Unfortunately, I no longer have the digital files for this project, but it was a pleasant surprise to find it intact after being stored for so many years.
