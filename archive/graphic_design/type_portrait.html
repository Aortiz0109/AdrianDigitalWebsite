Type Self-Portrait

Illustrator | 2010

This project focused on typography and the use of the type on path tool in Illustrator. I experimented with creating self-portraits by typing words over a reference photo. It resulted in a unique effect where the size of the text and path outlines formed the shape of the human figure. Initially, I created a black and white version, and later, I explored a color version by selecting colors from the photograph of my own face. The project allowed me to explore the intersection of typography and visual representation of the human form in an engaging and creative way.
