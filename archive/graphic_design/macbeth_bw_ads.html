Macbeth Black & White Ads

Theatre Ads | 2010

These three advertisements were part of two different projects that focused on creating advertisements for a function we could find on the Towson Events page, I picked the play Macbeth.

The Macbeth Ads project aimed to teach students about balance using simple forms. Each ad focused on a specific form: the first one utilized lines, the second one featured circles, and the final ad incorporated both lines and circles. The objective was to explore how these basic elements could create visual harmony and balance within the advertisements. It was a quick project that allowed students to experiment with different forms and understand the impact of balance in graphic design.
