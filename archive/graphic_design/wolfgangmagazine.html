Wolfgang Weingart Magazine

Magazine Cover & Spread | 2010

Indesign | Photoshop | Illustrator

For a typography class at AACC, I worked on a project centered around the typography work of Wolfgang Weingart. The task involved creating a cover spread and a two-page spread for a fictional magazine. I had studied Weingart's style and incorporated his influences into my designs. The final project was printed and displayed for presentation.
